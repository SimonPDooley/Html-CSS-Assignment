Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientists at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests, measuring performanceon simple games.

Our experiments always include a control condition('placebo') of the same underylying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes the difference.
